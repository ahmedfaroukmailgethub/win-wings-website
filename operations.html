End-to-End Process ✈︎

From Kitchen to Aircraft Galley

Every meal goes through a rigorous, multi-step process before it reaches the aircraft, ensuring safety, quality and compliance at every stage.

1

Procurement & Receiving

Ingredients are sourced from approved suppliers, received and inspected against quality and Halal standards before entering the production facility.

2

Production & Preparation

In-house kitchen production aligned with airline specifications, cabin class requirements and passenger dietary needs under continuous HACCP monitoring.

3

Quality Control & Packing

Each meal is inspected, certified and packed to aviation standards. Cold chain integrity is maintained throughout packing and loading operations.

4

Transport & Uplift

Time-critical delivery to the aircraft under airport security protocols. Our airport-based location ensures punctual uplift aligned with departure schedules.

5

Aircraft Loading & Cabin Setup

Final loading and galley setup on board, ensuring correct positioning, temperature and presentation for every cabin class before departure.
