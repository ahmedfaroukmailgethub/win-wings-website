Complete Service Range ✈︎

Every Service Above & Below the Clouds

WIN WINGS provides a comprehensive range of aviation catering and support services, designed to meet every airline, charter and operational requirement with uncompromising quality.

In-Flight Catering

Preparation and delivery of onboard meals for scheduled airline operations, meeting airline specifications and strict aviation standards.

  • Menu development & refinement
  • Full food safety & hygiene control
  • Final in-flight presentation
  • Aligned with partner budgets & requirements

VIP & Premium Class Catering

Premium in-flight dining solutions for Business and VIP Class passengers, where culinary excellence and presentation reflect the highest service expectations.

  • Executive chef-crafted menus
  • Restaurant-quality presentation
  • Fully compliant with airline specifications
  • Tailored to premium cabin standards

Charter Catering

Customized catering solutions for charter and non-scheduled flights, designed to enhance the passenger experience with creative culinary touches.

  • Business & Economy configurations
  • Partner menu concept alignment
  • Scalable and efficient execution
  • Memorable in-flight dining experience

Crew Catering

Dedicated catering solutions designed to support flight and cabin crews, recognizing their critical role in safe and continuous flight operations.

  • Carefully selected quality ingredients
  • Balanced & nutritious meals
  • Consistent presentation & taste
  • Uncompromising food safety standards

Transport & Handling

Professional transportation within the airport restricted zone, including full delivery to the aircraft and all related handling services, provided 24/7 for all aircraft types.

  • Hi-Loaders and Vehicles Transport
  • Logistics and related Handling Services
  • Complete traceability & reliability
  • Continuous operational readiness

Especial Services

Full Buy on Board and duty-free services supported by a customs-bonded warehouse, certified onboard payment systems and regulatory readiness.

  • Customs bonded warehouse storage
  • Certified duty-free handling procedures
  • In-Flight Off-Line Payment Solution
  • Buy-on-Board Solutions
